# Evidence Fusion Design & Ontology (Stage 8I)

This document establishes the mathematical and epistemic foundation for multi-evidence reasoning in ReconGraph V2.

## Phase 1: Evidence Pipeline Matrix (Architecture Audit)

An audit of the current canonical execution lifecycle (as enforced by ADR-008).

| Pipeline | Extraction | Interpretation | Projection | Contribution | Missingness | Contradiction |
|----------|------------|----------------|------------|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Vendor** | `VendorNameObservation` | `VendorPairInterpretation` | Similarity Score | `EvidenceContributionV2` | Silent (skipped if none) | Handled at Decision level |
| **Tax** | `TaxIdentifierArtifact` | `GSTINRelationState`, `PANRelationState` | Exact match scalar | `EvidenceContributionV2` | `ONE_MISSING`, `BOTH_MISSING` | Handled at Decision level |
| **Financial**| `FinancialArtifact` | `AmountRelationState` | Tolerance scalar | `EvidenceContributionV2` | Implicitly required | Handled at Decision level |
| **Reference**| `ReferenceIdentifierArtifact`| `ReferencePairInterpretation` | Exact/Fuzzy scalar | `EvidenceContributionV2` | Silent | Handled at Decision level |
| **Temporal** | `TemporalArtifact` | `TemporalPairInterpretation` | Within max days scalar| `EvidenceContributionV2` | Silent | Handled at Decision level |

**Current DecisionEngine Interaction:**
Currently, the `PairScorer` aggregates the evidence from all providers into a dictionary of scores. The `HypothesisEvaluator` loops over the providers, producing `SemanticFindings`. There is no formal fusion algebra—the final result relies on the `RelationshipPolicy` applying heuristics.

## Phase 2: Evidence Ontology

The canonical ontology representing the states of evidence throughout the execution lifecycle.

### `EvidenceObservation`
- **Purpose:** A deterministic, strongly-typed representation of raw extracted string data.
- **Invariants:** Must be reproducible. Must not contain any comparison logic.
- **Identity Rules:** Hashed from canonical attributes.

### `EvidenceInterpretation`
- **Purpose:** An epistemically honest statement of structural geometry between two observations.
- **Invariants:** Must not contain legal or semantic judgments (e.g., `SAME_COMPANY`). Must describe observable facts (e.g., `SAME_PAN_DIFFERENT_STATE`).
- **Identity Rules:** Uniquely identified by the tuple of Observation identities + context policy version.

### `EvidenceProjection`
- **Purpose:** A mathematical translation of Interpretation into a domain-specific continuous or categorical domain.
- **Invariants:** Must map strictly to `[0.0, 1.0]` or explicit distinct states.

### `EvidenceContribution`
- **Purpose:** The normalized, universal contract passed into the Fusion layer.
- **Invariants:** Must preserve lineage of the Projection. Must identify the provider.

### `EvidenceDependency`
- **Purpose:** Defines causal, structural, or ontological lineage between multiple pieces of Evidence. (e.g. `TaxEvidence` implicitly contains `VendorEvidence` via GSTIN lookup).
- **Invariants:** A DAG.

### `EvidenceContradiction`
- **Purpose:** A state where two independent `EvidenceContributions` suggest mutually exclusive realities.
- **Lifecycle:** Emerges ONLY at the Fusion layer.

### `EvidenceSupport`
- **Purpose:** The mathematical accumulation of aligned `EvidenceContributions`.

### `EvidenceMissingness`
- **Purpose:** The formal declaration of absence.

## Phase 7: Fusion Ontology

The objects responsible for graph-level multi-evidence reasoning.

### `EvidenceGraph`
- **Purpose:** A mathematical DAG representing the interactions, dependencies, and conflicts of all EvidenceContributions for a specific Candidate Hypothesis.

### `FusionNode`
- **Purpose:** A single vertex in the `EvidenceGraph` representing one Domain's `EvidenceContribution`.

### `FusionEdge`
- **Purpose:** A directed or undirected link between two `FusionNodes` indicating a Dependency, Corroboration, or Contradiction.

### `FusionPolicy`
- **Purpose:** The rules engine determining how support is accumulated across the `EvidenceGraph`.

### `FusionResult`
- **Purpose:** The final mathematical verdict (Belief, Compatibility, or Support) generated by the Fusion layer.

### `FusionExplanation`
- **Purpose:** The human-readable and machine-traceable artifact proving how the `FusionResult` was derived.

## Phase 8: Explanation Semantics

No explanation may invent reasoning. 

**The Traceability Invariant:**
Every statement in an Explanation MUST trace backward through the following chain:
`Decision` → `FusionResult` → `EvidenceContribution` → `EvidenceInterpretation` → `EvidenceObservation` → `Raw Record`.

If the Fusion layer reduces Support due to a Contradiction, the Explanation must explicitly reference the specific `FusionEdge` representing the conflict.

## Phase 9: Confidence Semantics

ReconGraph will **NOT** output "Confidence" or "Probability".

**Chosen Term: Compatibility**
- **Why:** "Probability" implies statistical frequency over an ensemble, which we don't have. "Confidence" implies epistemic certainty, which is legally dangerous.
- **Definition:** "Compatibility" represents the degree of structural alignment between two records as observed through independent deterministic pipelines, normalized against the dependency graph to prevent double-counting.
- **Mathematical Bound:** `[0.0, 1.0]`. A Compatibility of `1.0` means the records exhibit perfect structural alignment across all available dimensions without contradiction.
